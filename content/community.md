Clan is built by people who care about independent infrastructure and want it to be easier to run, share, and improve. The wider Clan community is where we coordinate work, help each other, and collect useful services that others can reuse.

## Join the conversation

The main place to reach us is the [Clan Matrix group](https://matrix.to/#/#clan:clan.lol). Stop by to ask questions, discuss ideas, share what you are building, or get pointed in the right direction.

## Office hours

We usually hold office hours on Thursdays at 17:00 Berlin time at [jitsi.clan.lol/pub](https://jitsi.clan.lol/pub). Office hours are currently temporarily paused, but this is the place to check once they resume.

## Community services

Community-maintained services live in the [clan-community repository](https://git.clan.lol/clan/clan-community). Please use these services if they are helpful, report what is missing, and contribute services you have written yourself so others can build on them too.
